Our Story

SOLARICH ENTERPRISES CORP.

Is a cutting-edge company that specializes in providing top-quality solar installation services and retail solutions to residential, commercial, and industrial clients. The company was founded in April 2022 (formerly known as Solaraze Enterprises Corp) and is proudly owned by Filipino shareholders who bring a wealth of experience and knowledge to the table.

Our Objectives

Solarich Enterprises Corporation

At Solarich, we recognize the importance of reducing our carbon footprint and helping clients achieve a greener way of living. With this ethos in mind we strive to deliver a comprehensive range of solar solutions that cater to the unique needs and requirements of each client.

We innovate and improve solutions for our clients by applying our high expertise in advanced materials and technology.

Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to delivering bespoke solar installations that exceed client expectations. We understand that every projecct is unique and require a tailored approach, which is why we work closely with clients to understand their specific needs and provide customized solutions.
