Our Story of Solar Innovation and Excellence

Our Mission

SOLARICH ENTERPRISES CORP.

Inspired and created with a business concept of Australian Renewable Energy, as the technical support for our solar products for Philippine market consumers. Solarich ECorp aims to develop and motivate local partners and oversee the service and maintenance process of the products and services.

Our Story

Solarich Enterprises Corporation

is a Philippine Company. It was founded in 2018 in Olongapo, Zambales, Philippines and expanded its operations in Cavite, Philippines to promote renewable energy in this country.

Our company provides solar solutions based on Australia Renewable Energy standards and concepts. Since 2018, we continue to partner with top Australian solar companies and brands around Victoria, Australia from key individuals who have long time experiences in the photovoltaic (PV) industry.

Our solar partners off shore are very well known solar services provider in the PV industry and have been working with the best PV panel manufacturer in Australia, Germany, Canada, etc.
